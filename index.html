About Jack

From there I started freelancing on Upwork and slowly built my portfolio until I was able to shift to full time Web Design and Development. I continue to love what I do and look forward to building more. When I'm not working at home I enjoy games. All kinds of games. Card games, board games, table top games, video games. You name it, I love it.
